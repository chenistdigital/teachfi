The problem

Entrepreneurship education has two big gaps

TeachFi solves both.

How it works

How the platform works for a school

  1. 01

    The school decides to implement TeachFi

    Building on the SRBan concept — validated for 5 years at Școala Româno-Britanică — the institution joins the ecosystem.

  2. 02

    The school locks TeachFi tokens

    The school acquires and locks a certain amount of TeachFi tokens for the entire duration of platform usage. They serve as the backing for its own currency.

  3. 03

    It issues its own token (e.g., SchoolY)

    The school mints its currency based on parity with TeachFi. It becomes the official internal token: own name, symbol and rules.

  4. 04

    Students earn tokens through activities

    Projects, milestones, jobs, volunteering, academic performance — all become income streams in the school's currency.

  5. 05

    They use the tokens across all platform modules

    Bank, marketplace, NFTs, events, classrooms, grants, services. Every transaction is a real economic lesson.

Curriculum

A 5-year program, validated through SRBan

The TeachFi middle-school curriculum runs across 5 school years (grades 5–9), from a student's first pocket money to real practice firms funded by their classmates.

Year 1

Grade 5

Money, budget, first income

Students build their first personal budget and earn their first income in SRBan tokens — promotional, merit and performance scholarships. They learn currencies, transactions, cards and accounts.

Year 2

Grade 6

Market, supply, demand, consumption

Students explore core economic concepts. The customized token becomes payment in the school market and at every sales event (Christmas and Easter fairs).

Year 3

Grade 7

Job, salary, CV, interviews

Students apply with CVs and motivation letters, sit for interviews and sign work contracts for internal jobs. Their first salary, earned through real work.

Year 4

Grade 8

Bank, savings, loans, interest

They open accounts, save and take loans — all in their own TeachFi Bank.

Year 5

Grade 9

Investments and practice firms

Students form teams and run real practice firms, providing products and services exclusively in the school token. A partner startup supplies the market of suppliers and clients.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How much does TeachFi cost a school?
Operating cost per student is near zero thanks to Solana's minimal fees. Our model is B2B: an annual school license + a per-student subscription.
Is it safe for minors to use a wallet and crypto?
Yes. School tokens are closed currencies — they cannot be traded on external exchanges or converted to real money without school approval. Student wallets are custodied at the school level under administration control. Parents have full visibility.
What about student data? GDPR?
TeachFi is strictly GDPR-compliant. Student personal data is stored encrypted in the school's database, not on chain. Only pseudonymous transactions live on chain — no information that directly identifies a student.
Do students need special hardware or technical knowledge?
No. The platform runs in any browser. The wallet is created automatically on first login. Students don't need to understand blockchain to use the platform — the interface looks like any modern app.
Which blockchain does TeachFi use?
Solana. We chose it for the near-zero fees (under $0.001 per transaction) and the fast confirmation time (~400ms). That means a school with 500 students can run thousands of transactions a day with no meaningful operational cost.
How long does it take to roll out at a new school?
Technical setup in 5 minutes. Curriculum configuration and teacher onboarding take 1–2 weeks. The SRBan pilot showed that students become operational within 1–2 hours of first use.
What is the LEARN token and when will it launch?
LEARN is TeachFi's governance token — still in development. It will enable exchange between school currencies and, through regulated mechanisms, fund alumni's first startups via the launchpad. Launch is planned alongside the regional expansion phase.
Are the tokens "real money"?
School tokens are educational currencies, not regulated payment instruments. They cannot be directly converted into real money. The LEARN token, when launched, will have a separate regulated component — exclusively for the alumni launchpad.
