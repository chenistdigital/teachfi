The Marketing Services module gives students real-agency tools: in-platform banners, promoted messages, campaign analytics. They pay with tokens and see the return on their investment directly.

It is a crash course in performance marketing without the jargon. They learn ROI, CTR, conversion and A/B testing — on their own practice firm.
