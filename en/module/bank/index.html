TeachFi Bank is the pilot module of the entire ecosystem. Students open accounts, deposit savings, earn interest (2–10%), take loans and learn what credit scoring really means — first-hand, but with zero real risk.

In grades 7 and 8, the bank becomes the instrument students use to invest in their classmates' practice firms. They become shareholders, receive dividends and review performance.

Every operation is a smart contract on Solana — auditable, transparent and almost free to run.
