NFT Marketplace turns students' creative projects into digital assets on chain. Paintings, 3D models, photographs, music compositions, code artifacts — everything can be tokenized as a unique NFT and traded internally.

The module provides a complete decentralized marketplace: minting, listing, bidding, escrow and transfer. Students learn what digital ownership, copyright, scarcity and secondary markets actually mean.
