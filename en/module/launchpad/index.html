Launchpad is the crown module of the TeachFi ecosystem — still under development. Alumni who have demonstrated 5 years of economic, financial and entrepreneurial performance get to pitch their real projects.

Backing comes in the form of LEARN tokens from classmates, parents, alumni and partner investors. That backing is converted, through regulated mechanisms, into real startup capital.

This is how TeachFi becomes the world's first pre-university accelerator.
