Each school creates its own token through a wizard with clear steps: name, symbol, total supply, locking mechanism, transfer rules. In minutes the token is live on chain.

The model is inspired by the SRBan pilot — a token validated for 5 years at Școala Româno-Britanică. It proved that a well-designed internal currency can transform student economic behavior.

The token stays 100% under school control. TeachFi provides the infrastructure; the rules are defined locally.
