On Ramp SRBan is the module that bridges the real world to the TeachFi ecosystem. Parents, schools, sponsors and alumni can purchase TeachFi tokens or convert other cryptocurrencies into the school's customized token.

It is the official entry point — the first time real money becomes digital value in the classroom. Conversion is handled by a dedicated smart contract with transparent rates and a complete on-chain history.

For students, the on-ramp stays almost invisible — they just see tokens appear in their wallet. For the school, it is a real financial tool with reporting and auditing built in.
