Freelancer Market is an internal mini-Upwork. Students list their skills, receive requests, deliver, get paid in tokens and collect ratings. They learn what a portfolio, a commercial proposal, a timeline and client management actually involve.

The module includes automatic escrow: tokens are locked until the client confirms delivery, eliminating disputes.
