Goods Market is the school's internal bourse. Students sell physical products made in workshops, second-hand items, candies from fairs, or digital goods (templates, assets, beats).

The module handles catalog, search, orders, delivery and payment smart contracts. At Christmas and Easter fairs — key moments in the curriculum — it becomes the main economic tool.
