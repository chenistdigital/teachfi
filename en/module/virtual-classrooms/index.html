Virtual Classrooms turns the token into a way to access online courses — from advanced math to coding, public speaking or music. Courses can be created by teachers, by older students, or by partner schools in the ecosystem.

Token-based payment means a popular course generates income for its creator and for the host school. It is a real knowledge economy.
