Event Access tokenizes tickets for any educational event — workshops, conferences, clubs, summer camps. Each ticket is a unique NFT, impossible to forge and easy to transfer.

Students reserve their seats by spending earned tokens. That creates a real opportunity cost: if they attend the coding workshop, they can't also buy from the marketplace. They learn to prioritize their budget.
