METAVERSE School is TeachFi's long-term vision: an international school in virtual space where students from all over the world meet, learn together and build projects.

It uses VR and AR for immersive lessons: virtual labs, museum visits, historical simulations, 3D design workshops. The token becomes the base currency for accessing premium spaces.
